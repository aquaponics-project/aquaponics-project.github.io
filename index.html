Product Information

Aquaponics @ Home is a beacon for sustainable living, offering a simple, quick to assemble aquaponics kit that’s perfect for any home. Say goodbye to complex setups; this system is designed for effortless assembly, making aquaponics accessible to all.

The system comprises just four components: a fish tank, an NFT rack, a combined water pump and replaceable filter, and an air pump. This setup allows it to be quickly assembled, hassle-free.

Aquaponics @ Home isn’t just about growing plants—it’s about embracing a lifestyle of eco-consciousness, even if you are confined to the boundaries of an urban apartment. Enjoy the flavour of home-grown food while observing your fish swim in the tank.

Join the movement towards a sustainable future with Aquaponics @ Home, where simplicity meets sustainability in the comfort of your living room.

A beacon to a greener future

Utilizing Nutrient Film Technique (NFT), plants receive a continuous thin film of water, delivering nutrients, while allowing oxygen to reach their roots. It also prevents algal growth as the water is flowing.
